Ratan Tata

1937 - Present

Summary

Born: 28 December 1937

Education: B. Arch Cornell University

Awarded Padma bhushan by Government of India

Life Timeline

  • Graduated from Riverdale Country School, New York City in 1955
  • Joined the Tata Group in 1962
  • Appointed, Director-in-charge of Nelco in 1971
  • Became Chairman of Tata Industries in 1991

Social Work

  1. Endowed a $28 million Tata Scholarship Fund, allowing Cornel University to provide financial aid to undergraduate students in India
  2. Donated $50 million for construction of an executive centre at Harvard Business School
  3. Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) gave $35 million, to Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) for a facility to research in cognitive systems and autonomous vehicles
